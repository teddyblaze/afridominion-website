De-Risking the Supply Chain

Sovereign Risk Mitigation

Afridominion navigates the complex landscape of local African regulatory environments so that your global platform doesn't have to. We act as the buffer between intricate local laws, royalty frameworks, and export permitting, and your digital operations. This de-risking capability is what allows clean tokenization to scale across borders without friction.

We understand the nuances of sovereign regulations, we maintain relationships with governmental bodies, and we manage the compliance infrastructure that transforms localized gold into globally-tradeable assets.

  • Gold Trade Acts adherence
  • Cross-border financial regulations
  • Export permit management
  • Royalty and taxation compliance
  • Notice of Pledge agreements

ESG Reporting & Transparency

  • Blockchain-ready audit reporting
  • Real-time vault-to-platform data integration
  • Third-party verification (Bureau Veritas / SGS)
  • LBMA benchmark compliance

Our commitment to ESG excellence extends beyond compliance - it is embedded in our reporting infrastructure. We deliver blockchain-ready audit reporting that meets the highest ethical standards the world demands. Real-time data integration bridges vault logs with the digital platform, creating an immutable audit trail of every transaction.

Third-party verification through Bureau Veritas or SGS ensures independent validation of all ESG claims. This means investors, partners, and stakeholders have absolute confidence in the ethical provenance of every ounce of gold.

We don't just find gold; we make gold liquid, digital, and global - while ensuring every ounce meets the highest ethical standards the world demands.
