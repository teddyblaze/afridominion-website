The Opportunity

While gold remains the world's premier hedge against inflation, the physical trade is plagued by high barriers to entry, logistical friction, and lack of transparency. Afridominion Logistics Ltd acts as the Strategic Enabler, providing the physical infrastructure required to back digital tokenization platforms.

Our Enabler Model

  • +Asset Sourcing - vetting providers in African jurisdictions
  • +Security Infrastructure - Tier-1 banks and refiners
  • +Liquidity Management - weekly LBMA-based payouts

Financial Logic

Revenue Model

Diversified income from transaction fees, management fees, and spread on gold movements.

Efficiency

50/50 profit-share after GAAP expenses between Digital Engine and Physical Engine.

Scalability

Replicable framework designed to expand from Zimbabwe into SADC and West Africa within 24 months.

Investor FAQs

Triple-Lock process - Assay at government-approved refineries, weekly audits by independent third parties, immediate transfer to bank-grade vaults with 24/7 security and insurance.

In-Situ Institutional Custody with national banks (ZB Bank, CABS). Legal "Notice of Pledge" agreements ensure gold is earmarked as underlying asset for Uhuru HK's global subscribers.

Afridominion manages the "Liquidity Bridge" - weekly payments based on LBMA Gold Price through transparent banking channels, preventing supply-side hoarding.

GAAP-based. Gross revenue collected, verifiable operational expenses deducted first, remaining net profit split equally between Digital Engine (Uhuru) and Physical Engine (Afridominion).

Non-exclusive. Allows agility for multiple platforms/ETFs. Strong NCND clauses protect deal integrity.

Under Laws of Hong Kong. Arbitration via International Chamber of Commerce (ICC) rules.

Afridominion doesn't just find gold; we make gold liquid, digital, and global. By managing the physical risk in Africa, we unlock the digital reward in Hong Kong and the GCC.
