Compliance

Our commitment to regulatory excellence across every jurisdiction we operate in

Our Compliance Philosophy

At Afridominion, compliance is not simply a box-ticking exercise - it is foundational to our mission. Operating at the intersection of African mining, global finance, and blockchain technology requires a rigorous, multi-jurisdictional approach to regulatory adherence. We believe that trust is earned through transparency, and our compliance framework reflects that commitment at every level of our operations.

Compliance Framework

The pillars that govern our operations across all markets

KYC / AML

Comprehensive Know Your Customer and Anti-Money Laundering procedures applied to all platform participants. Multi-layer identity verification ensures that every stakeholder - from artisanal miners to institutional investors - is properly vetted before engagement.

Multi-Jurisdictional

We operate under the laws of Hong Kong while maintaining compliance with the regulatory frameworks of Zimbabwe, South Africa, and other African nations where our Enablers operate. Our legal structure is designed to meet the requirements of each jurisdiction simultaneously.

Audit Trail

Every transaction, custody transfer, and tokenization event is recorded with an immutable audit trail. Blockchain-based record-keeping provides regulators and stakeholders with full traceability from mine site to digital asset issuance.

Regulatory Standards We Adhere To

Hong Kong Regulatory Framework

As a Hong Kong-registered entity, Afridominion Logistics Ltd. operates under the oversight of Hong Kong's regulatory environment. We comply with the Anti-Money Laundering and Counter-Terrorist Financing Ordinance (AMLO), the Companies Ordinance, and applicable Securities and Futures Commission (SFC) guidelines where our activities intersect with regulated financial services.

African Mining Regulations

Our Enabler network operates within the mining regulatory frameworks of their respective countries. In Zimbabwe, this includes compliance with the Mines and Minerals Act, the Zimbabwe Mining Development Corporation requirements, and the Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe's gold trading regulations. We require all Enablers to maintain valid mining licenses and permits as a condition of participation.

International Standards

Afridominion aligns with international best practices including the LBMA Responsible Gold Guidance, the OECD Due Diligence Guidance for Responsible Supply Chains of Minerals from Conflict-Affected and High-Risk Areas, and the Financial Action Task Force (FATF) Recommendations on combating money laundering and terrorist financing.

Data Protection

We comply with the Hong Kong Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance and implement data protection standards consistent with the GDPR for our international users. Full details of our data handling practices are available in our Privacy Policy.

Sanctions Screening & Due Diligence

All platform participants are screened against international sanctions lists, including the United Nations Security Council Consolidated List, the US Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) Specially Designated Nationals List, the EU Consolidated List of Sanctions, and the Hong Kong Monetary Authority designated lists.

We perform ongoing monitoring and periodic re-screening to ensure continued compliance. Any matches or potential concerns trigger an enhanced due diligence process managed by our compliance team.

Conflict Minerals

Afridominion maintains a zero-tolerance policy regarding conflict minerals. Our supply chain due diligence process is designed to prevent gold from conflict-affected regions or illegal sources from entering our ecosystem. Every ounce of gold processed through our platform is traced from its origin, and our ESG framework includes on-site verification by approved auditors. For full details, see our ESG Standards.

Reporting & Whistleblower Protection

Afridominion encourages the reporting of any suspected compliance violations, fraud, or unethical conduct. We provide secure channels for reporting and are committed to protecting whistleblowers from retaliation.

If you become aware of any activity that may violate our compliance policies, applicable laws, or ethical standards, please contact our compliance team:

Compliance Department
Email: compliance@afridominion.com

All reports are treated with strict confidentiality and investigated thoroughly by our compliance team. Where appropriate, findings are escalated to relevant regulatory authorities.

Continuous Improvement

The regulatory landscape for gold trading, digital assets, and cross-border commerce is evolving rapidly. Afridominion is committed to staying ahead of regulatory developments and continuously strengthening our compliance framework. Our compliance team regularly reviews and updates our policies, procedures, and controls to reflect current best practices and regulatory expectations.

For questions about our compliance program, contact us at compliance@afridominion.com.
